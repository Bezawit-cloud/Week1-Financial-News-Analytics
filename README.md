# Week1-Financial-News-Analytics

## Overview
This project analyzes financial news and analyst ratings data to extract insights about market trends, stock performance, and sentiment. The dataset includes news articles, analyst ratings, and price targets, mainly covering earnings and stock upgrades/downgrades.

---

## Key Insights from Week 1

- The dataset is heavily financial-focused, mainly covering **stocks, earnings, and analyst updates**.  
- Much of the content revolves around **actionable events** such as upgrades, downgrades, and price targets.  
- Certain news sources, such as **Lisa Levin** and **Benzinga Newsdesk**, drove the **2020 surge**, likely due to frequent reporting on earnings and market-moving events.  

**Trends over time:**

- From **2012 to 2019**, the news volume trend was smooth and steady.  
- Starting **late 2019**, the article count began increasing.  
- By **2020**, there was a **sharp rise** in the number of articles published.  

**Major contributors to the 2020 spike:**

- **Lisa Levin** and **Benzinga Newsdesk** published significantly more articles starting late 2019–2020.  
- They were the main drivers of the sharp increase in news volume.  

These insights help identify **key sources and events** that influence market movements, which is crucial for investors and analysts.

---

## Features
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of financial news.  
- Data cleaning and preprocessing for missing/inconsistent data.  
- Visualization of key financial metrics and analyst ratings.  
- Trend analysis and insights on stock price movements based on news and analyst sentiment.
